My partner was Jordan Hendley, and we used my Repo for the source code.

The game is complete, except for bonus features like sound, or fancy modifiers. We implemented all of the pieces, they rotate completely, and you can clear up to 4 lines at a time. When lines are cleared, the remaining blocks fall to take the place of what was there before.

Regarding AJAX, we have a High Score table that, if you beat at least the lowest score, you can add your name to the list. It is served on page load, and dynamically added to and refreshed when you add your name if you get a high score. The game is generated and a new game is generated with an AJAX call as well.
